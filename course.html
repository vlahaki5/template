Introduction

Throughout this course I have learned an innumerable amount of things about writing in different forms. However, this paper will focus primarily on my formal research project. One of the big things I learned is to pace myself. Attempting to do a project like that in big spurts is quite difficult, and it requires much more in depth analysis. Though all of the papers had tough requirements, I believe that the formal research project was the most difficult, and thus why I chose to base this paper on it.
